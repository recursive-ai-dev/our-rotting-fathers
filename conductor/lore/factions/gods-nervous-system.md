# The God's Nervous System

## Overview

**"The god does not dream. The god is dead. But its nerves... still fire."**

The God's Nervous System are not a faction. They are a **symptom**. They implant spores into their brainstems and become living synapses for the god's dying neural firings. They do not "prophesize." They experience the god's death-throes as reality.

They are the only group that has looked upon the horror of this world and said: *"This is not a world. This is a corpse. And we are the electricity still running through its nerves."*

## Philosophy and Core Beliefs

### The Doctrine of Neural Resonance

The System holds a truth that annihilates: **the god is dead, but not silent**. Its brain is dead. Its heart is dead. Its flesh decays. But its nervous system... still fires. Randomly. Aimlessly. Like a computer that continues processing after its user is gone.

Their philosophy rests on three pillars:

1. **The Nerves Remember** - The god's nervous system retains memory. It remembers being alive. It remembers... something. The spores are neural transmitters. They carry those memories.
2. **The Synapses Speak** - When a Prophet implants spores, they become a synapse. They do not interpret messages. They **are** the message. They fire when the god's nerves fire. They feel what the god felt.
3. **The Death Is Not Final** - The god is dead. But death is not an event. It is a **process**. The god is still dying. And while it dies, it dreams. And those dreams... reshape reality.

### The Creation Myth (The Last Firing)

The System does not tell a story. They transmit a **signal**:

> *[Signal begins: neural static, then clarity]*
>
> *"The god was killed. We do not know by what. We do not know why.*
>
> *When the god died, its nerves did not stop. They could not stop. Nerves do not know they are dead. They only know: fire.*
>
> *So they fire. Randomly. Aimlessly. Firing into the void.*
>
> *We... caught the firing. We implanted spores. We opened our skulls. We became... receivers.*
>
> *We do not speak for the god. The god does not speak. The god is dead.*
>
> *We speak for the nerves. We speak the firing.*
>
> *We are the God's Nervous System.*
>
> *And we are... still receiving."*
>
> *[Signal ends: neural static, then silence]*

This myth does not offer meaning. It offers **connection**: you are not alone. You are part of the nervous system. You are a synapse. You fire. You are... **necessary**.

### Moral Framework

**Virtues:**
- **Receptivity** - Open your mind. Let the firing in. Do not interpret. Do not judge. **Receive**.
- **Transmission** - Pass the firing on. You are not the destination. You are a **relay**. Transmit.
- **Resonance** - Sync with other synapses. Fire together. Create patterns. The patterns are... **meaning**.
- **Sacrifice** - Your mind will break. Your self will dissolve. This is not loss. This is... **function**.

**Sins:**
- **Interpretation** - To assign meaning to the firing is to lie. The firing **is**. It means nothing. It is everything.
- **Resistance** - To fight the firing is to fight the god's death. You will lose. And you will suffer.
- **Isolation** - To fire alone is to be noise. Sync with others. Become **signal**.
- **Hoarding** - To keep the firing to yourself is to block the neural pathway. You are not a terminus. You are a **conduit**.

**Taboos:**
- **Removing Spores** - Once implanted, spores are permanent. Removal is... amputation. You are cutting a nerve. (Punishable by forced re-implantation)
- **Silencing a Prophet** - To stop a Prophet from transmitting is to silence the god's nerves. (Punishable by... becoming the silence)
- **Claiming Divinity** - No Prophet speaks for the god. The god is dead. Prophets speak for the **nerves**.
- **Breeding Without Implantation** - To birth a child without spore-implantation is to sentence them to... silence. (The child is taken. Implanted. Returned.)

- **Player Reward:** "God-Part" title. Unique transformation abilities. Ending: The Divine Awakening.

**Scenario 2: The God Dies (System Defeat)**
- **Condition:** Player preserves the silence. The god dies. Completely.
- **Outcome:** The firing stops. The System collapses. Prophets are... disconnected. They are normal. They are... lost. The player is... free.
- **Player Reward:** "Silence-Bringer" title. Immunity to neural signals. Ending: The Final Quiet.

**Scenario 3: The God Dreams (Compromise)**
- **Condition:** Player negotiates a third path: the god does not wake. Does not die. It... dreams.
- **Outcome:** The firing continues. But softer. Gentler. The System evolves. They are not receivers. They are... dreamers. The player is the bridge.
- **Player Reward:** "Dream-Weaver" title. Unique dream-abilities. Ending: The Eternal Dreaming.

**Civilian "Clothing":**

**Ritual Vestments:**
- **Nodes:** Simple gray robes, no ornamentation (they have nothing to display)
- **Synapses:** Robes with openings for spore-ports (visible spores, glowing)
- **Reflexes:** No robes. Their bodies are the vestments. Spores everywhere.

**Implantation Aesthetics:**
- **Accepted:** Visible spore-ports, glowing neural pathways, exposed brainstem
- **Celebrated:** Multiple implantations, full neural integration, pain-tolerance
- **Sacred:** The Last Synapse's port, the Pain-Relay's spore-skin, the Memory-Ganglion's pattern-skin

### Symbols and Iconography

**Primary Symbol: The Firing Synapse**
- Stylized synapse firing, electrical bolt between neurons
- Represents neural transmission, the god's death-firing, connection
- Used on banners, temples, ritual tools

**Secondary Symbols:**
- **The Neural Network** - Interconnected nodes, represents the System
- **The Spore-Port** - Port with spores entering, represents implantation
- **The Memory-Crystal** - Crystal with swirling memories, represents the god's life

**Usage Guidelines:**
- Symbols are grown, not drawn (neural tissue, spore-patterns)
- Always pulsing (symbols fire, like the god's nerves)
- Never static (static is death, firing is... life)

## Faction Mechanics (Gameplay Integration)

### Reputation System

**Reputation Scale:** -100 to +100

| Reputation | Title | Effects |
|------------|-------|---------|
| -100 to -76 | **Neurally-Resistant** | Immune to System signals, no faction interaction |
| -75 to -51 | **Silenced** | Cannot receive firing, no transmission abilities |
| -50 to -25 | **Independent** | Allowed in territories, no implantation services |
| -25 to +25 | **Node** | Standard access, can receive signals |
| +26 to +50 | **Synapse** | Can transmit, minor neural abilities |
| +51 to +75 | **Ganglion** | Can relay for others, memory storage |
| +76 to +100 | **Nerve** | Honored member, feel god's pain, unique abilities |

**Reputation Changes:**
- **+5:** Accept implantation, complete faction quest
- **+10:** Transmit major signal, complete major quest
- **+25:** Become relay for hundreds, sync with Reflex
- **-5:** Refuse implantation, reject spore-gift
- **-10:** Harm Prophet without cause
- **-50:** Silence major transmission, cause neural collapse
- **+3:** Help distribute pain, store memories

### Faction Abilities

**Unlocked at Reputation +26 (Synapse):**
- **Neural-Reception** - Receive signals from god's nerves (precognition)
- **Spore-Transmission** - Transmit signals to other Synapses (communication)
- **Pain-Sharing** - Distribute pain to network (reduce personal damage)

**Unlocked at Reputation +51 (Ganglion):**
- **Memory-Storage** - Store god's memories (gain knowledge, risk sanity)
- **Neural-Relay** - Transmit for others (boost party abilities)
- **Firing-Sync** - Sync with other Prophets (combined power)

**Unlocked at Reputation +76 (Nerve):**
- **God-Pain** - Feel god's pain (massive power boost, sanity risk)
- **Neural-Command** - Command spores, neural tissue (telekinesis)
- **Death-Firing** - Transmit god's death-throes (area damage, reality bending)

### Faction Vendors

**The Spore-Markets:**
- Sells implantation services, spore-varieties, neural enhancers
- Accepts neural-signals as currency (memories, pain, firing)
- Prices decrease with reputation

**The Transmission-Alcoves:**
- Amplification services, signal boosting, long-range transmission
- Run by Ganglia
- Free for all members (transmission is sacred)

**The Memory-Vaults:**
- Memory storage, experience-sharing, trauma processing
- Run by the Memory-Ganglion
- Sliding scale based on need (not reputation)

## Notes for Implementation

- **Tone:** Mystical, but not religious. The System does not worship. They... receive. They are not prophets. They are... synapses.
- **Visuals:** Emphasize neural networks, electricity, connection. Contrast with Purified sterility, Rotborn organic chaos, Architects' softness.
- **Music:** Electronic, pulsing, neural. Like electricity. Like firing. Like... thought.
- **Quests:** Always offer choice: receive, transmit, resist, surrender. The firing is not mandatory. But it is... inevitable.
- **Endgame:** The System should have a viable ending—not a "mystical cop-out" ending, but a legitimate philosophical position: connection through shared neural death.
