# Our Rotting Father RPG - Product Guidelines

## 1. Prose & Writing Style

### Core Principle: Gritty Hallucination
The writing style dynamically shifts between two modes based on the player character's psychosis level:

#### Low Sanity Mode (Gritty/Direct)
- **Characteristics**: Raw, visceral, unflinching descriptions
- **Sentence Structure**: Short, punchy, immediate
- **Word Choice**: Concrete, physical, emphasizing decay and sensation
- **Example**: *"The wound opens. Pus and black fluid seep through the bandage. The smell hits first—sweet rot, like fruit left in sun. You've smelled it before. On your mother's arm. Before the priests took her to the Purification Pits."*

#### High Sanity Mode (Hallucinatory/Dreamlike)
- **Characteristics**: Flowing, ambiguous, reality-blurring
- **Sentence Structure**: Longer, winding, recursive
- **Word Choice**: Abstract, sensory, emphasizing movement and transformation
- **Example**: *"The walls breathe. You watch them—ribcage of the world, expanding, contracting. Or is it your own chest? The distinction dissolves like meat in acid. A whisper: not from outside, never from outside. The god dreams and you are the dream. Wake. Sleep. Die. Live."*

### Dialogue Guidelines
- **Faction Dialects**: Each faction has distinct speech patterns:
  - **Purified**: Clinical, precise, avoid rot-related metaphors
  - **Rotborn**: Ecstatic, prophetic, embrace body horror language
  - **Council**: Diplomatic, measured, use euphemisms for madness
  - **Prophets**: Cryptic, poetic, speak in riddles and visions
- **Unreliable Narration**: NPCs may contradict each other; player must choose what to believe
- **Psychosis Effects**: At high sanity, dialogue options expand but may be delusional

### Naming Conventions
- **Characters**: Melodic but slightly off (e.g., "Yh'loth," "Mira the Unbound," "Father-Carrion")
- **Places**: Anatomical + spiritual (e.g., "The Weeping Marrow," "Sanctuary of the Last Breath")
- **Items**: Descriptive + lore (e.g., "Bone-Whisper Dagger," "Spore-Touched Robes")
- **Avoid**: Modern slang, anachronistic references, generic fantasy names

## 2. Visual Art Direction

### Dark Pixel Art Aesthetic

#### Core Principles
1. **High Detail, Low Resolution**: Crisp pixel art with meticulous animation
2. **Atmospheric Lighting**: Dynamic shadows, god-rays through spore clouds
3. **Body Horror Subtlety**: Suggest rather than show—silhouettes, implications
4. **Color Psychology**: Palette shifts reflect sanity and location

#### Color Palettes
- **The Withered Hand** (Starting Area): Desaturated browns, grays, sickly yellows
- **The Festering Heart**: Deep reds, purples, bioluminescent greens
- **The Bone Spires**: Stark whites, pale blues, harsh contrasts
- **The Dreaming Mind**: Surreal, shifting colors, impossible gradients

#### Character Design
- **Protagonist**: Customizable but always shows signs of rot-exposure (scars, discoloration, bandages)
- **NPCs**: Distinct silhouettes, faction-coded clothing and mutations
- **Enemies**: Creative mutations—avoid generic "zombie" tropes
- **Animation**: Fluid but slightly wrong—uncanny valley as intentional effect

#### Environmental Storytelling
- Architecture reflects faction values (Purified = angular, clean; Rotborn = organic, flowing)
- Debris and remains suggest history without explicit exposition
- Hidden details reward exploration (frescoes, carvings, mass graves)

#### UI Integration
- **Health Bar**: Visualized as character's blood/rot level
- **Sanity Meter**: Represented as cracking/breaking effect on screen edges
- **Inventory**: Displayed as physical items in a satchel view
- **Maps**: Hand-drawn on parchment that degrades with exposure

## 3. Audio Design

### Minimalist/Unsettling Approach

#### Core Philosophy
- **Less is More**: Silence is a weapon; use it strategically
- **Organic Textures**: All sounds should feel biological or environmental
- **Psychosis Audio**: Sound design changes based on sanity level

#### Sound Categories

##### Ambient Soundscapes
- **Constant Layers**: Dripping, creaking, distant breathing, wind through bone
- **Location-Specific**: Each area has 3-5 unique ambient layers
- **Dynamic Mixing**: Layers fade in/out based on player position and sanity

##### Sound Effects
- **Combat**: Impactful but not gratuitous—focus on weight, not gore
- **Magic**: Unsettling frequencies, reversed audio, whale songs, bone cracks
- **UI Sounds**: Organic clicks (bone taps, flesh presses), not mechanical

##### Music
- **Minimal Leitmotifs**: 3-5 core themes, heavily varied
- **Instrumentation**: Strings (cello, bass), percussion (bones, skin drums), choir (wordless)
- **Adaptive System**: 
  - Exploration: Sparse, ambient drones
  - Combat: Rhythmic, percussive, building intensity
  - Story Beats: Full orchestral, but only for key moments

##### Psychoacoustic Effects
- **High Sanity**: Introduce binaural beats, infrasound, whispered voices
- **Directional Ambiguity**: Sounds that seem to come from from everywhere/nowhere
- **Accessibility**: Option to disable disorienting effects

## 4. UI/UX Design

### Diegetic/Organic Philosophy

#### Core Principle
The UI should feel like it exists *within* the world, not overlaid on top of it.

#### Implementation

##### Health & Status Display
- **Visual**: Character model shows visible wounds, bandages, mutations
- **HUD Option**: Minimalist overlay styled as carved bone tablets
- **Sanity Indicator**: Screen edges crack, warp, or pulse with corruption

##### Inventory Management
- **Presentation**: Physical satchel view with item icons
- **Interaction**: Drag-and-drop, items can be examined (rotated, inspected)
- **Descriptions**: Handwritten notes attached to items

##### Menus & Navigation
- **Pause Menu**: Appears as ritual circle or mystical diagram
- **Quest Log**: Journal with handwritten entries that degrade over time
- **Map**: Parchment that burns/rots at edges in high-exposure areas

##### Dialogue System
- **Portrait System**: NPC portraits show emotional state and mutation level
- **Choice Display**: Text appears as if carved or written in real-time
- **Psychosis Effects**: At high sanity, some options may be hidden or illusory

#### Accessibility Considerations
- **Toggle Options**: All diegetic effects can be disabled for clarity
- **Colorblind Modes**: Alternative indicators beyond color (symbols, patterns)
- **Text Scaling**: Support 100%-200% without breaking layout
- **Subtitle Options**: Speaker identification, tone indicators, content warnings

## 5. Branding Guidelines

### Logo & Title Treatment
- **Primary Logo**: "Our Rotting Father" in custom lettering that appears to decay
- **Subtitle**: "RPG" minimal, secondary
- **Usage**: Always on dark/organic background, never on pure white

### Marketing Materials
- **Tone**: Mysterious, atmospheric, thought-provoking
- **Imagery**: Focus on scale (the god-corpse world) and choice (faction conflict)
- **Taglines**: 
  - *"Born on a corpse. Blessed by decay."*
  - *"Your madness is your weapon."*
  - *"What will you become when the god awakens?"*

### Community Guidelines
- **Inclusive Language**: Mental health portrayed respectfully, not as punchline
- **Content Warnings**: Clear labeling of body horror, psychological themes
- **Moderation**: Zero tolerance for harassment, hate speech, ableism

---

## 6. Quality Standards

### Performance Targets
- **Frame Rate**: Stable 60 FPS on target hardware
- **Load Times**: <5s initial, <2s transitions
- **Memory**: <500MB RAM usage for browser compatibility

### Polish Requirements
- **No Placeholder Art**: All assets final quality before release
- **Consistent Style**: Every asset reviewed against art bible
- **Bug-Free Core**: Combat, saves, progression must be flawless

### Playtesting Metrics
- **First Hour**: Must hook player with story + combat loop
- **Difficulty Curve**: Gradual introduction of mechanics
- **Save System**: Never lose more than 5 minutes of progress
